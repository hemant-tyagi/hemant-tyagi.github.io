Research Interests

Summary of research
Broadly speaking, I am interested in inverse problems where we are given noisy and limited information about a signal of interest, and the goal is to estimate (or "learn") the unknown signal. I am particularly interested in the setting where the signal is high-dimensional, but has some intrinsic low-dimensional structure. So far, I have worked on the following problems.

- Learning high dimensional functions from point queries
- Multi-armed bandit problems
- Super-resolution of point sources
- Phase unwrapping
- Inverse problems on graphs (community detection, ranking from pairwise comparisons, group synchronization, graph matching)
- Learning dynamical systems
