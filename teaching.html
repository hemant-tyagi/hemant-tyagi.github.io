Teaching

  • Statistics I (M1 Data Science, École Centrale de Lille): Fall 2020, Fall 2021. This course provides an introduction to the basics of statistics. Topics covered include basics of probability, descriptive statistics, confidence intervals, hypothesis testing, parametric and nonparametric tests.

  • Statistics II (M1 Data Science, École Centrale de Lille): Spring 2021, Spring 2022 This is relatively more advanced than Statistics I and focuses on elements of theoretical statistics. Topics covered include exponential families, maximum likelihood estimation, Fisher Information, M-estimators, Neyman-Pearson lemma.
